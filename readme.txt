== Description ==

**Mega Navify**: *Elevate Your Website Navigation!*

Transform your website’s navigation experience with **Mega Navify**, the ultimate mega menu plugin designed to seamlessly integrate with your existing theme. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced developer, our intuitive interface makes it easy to create stunning menus that enhance user engagement.

**Mega Navify** is a user-friendly WordPress plugin that helps you create beautiful mega menus for your website. It works perfectly with your current theme and is easy to set up!

= Key Features of Mega Navify =

* **Works with Your Existing Theme**: Mega Navify is compatible with any WordPress theme, allowing you to elevate your site's navigation without sacrificing your design.
* **Drag & Drop Mega Menu Builder**: Create your perfect menu effortlessly with our user-friendly drag-and-drop builder. Customize your layout with ease!
* **No CSS Knowledge Required**: You don’t need to be a coding expert! Build and customize your mega menus without any CSS skills.
* **Display WordPress Widgets in Your Menu**: Enhance your menus by incorporating WordPress widgets, making your navigation more interactive and informative.
* **Supports Flyout and Mega Menu Styles**: Choose between traditional flyout menus or expansive mega menus to suit your site’s style and functionality.
* **Multiple Menus with Unique Configurations**: Manage multiple menus, each with its own settings, to cater to different areas of your website.
* **Hover Intent, Hover, or Click Events**: Flexibly choose how your submenus open—via hover intent, a simple hover, or a click event for a tailored user experience.
* **Advanced Icon Selection Interface**: Easily add icons to your menu items with our advanced selection interface, allowing for a visually appealing navigation setup.
* **Flexible Menu Location Creator**: Customize where your menus appear on your site, providing you with the flexibility to enhance your site’s layout.

*Upgrade your website’s navigation today with Mega Navify and give your visitors the seamless browsing experience they deserve!*

== Installation ==
Installing the plugin is easy. Just follow these steps:

1. From the dashboard of your site, navigate to Plugins --> Add New.
2. Select the Upload option and hit "Choose File."
3. When the popup appears select the mega-navify.zip file from your desktop.
4. Follow the on-screen instructions and wait till the upload completes.
5. When it's finished, activate the plugin via the prompt. A message will display confirming activation was successful.

= What is Mega Navify? =

Mega Navify is a WordPress plugin that allows you to create beautiful and customizable mega menus for your website without any coding skills.

No, Mega Navify is designed for users of all skill levels. The drag-and-drop builder makes it easy to create menus without coding.
